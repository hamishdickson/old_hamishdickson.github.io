More about me

What interests me

I've recently finished Andrew Ng's excellent Machine Learning coursera course. I found this fascinating and would love to know more or work on an interesting machine learning problem (I've been trying to find something open source to contribute to, but so far haven't found anything that catches my eye).

I'd really like to know more about genetics, how the brain works and biology in general - I think this area has a lot of untapped potential for interesting maths and computer modelling.

Turbulence and fluid dynamics are very interesting to me, we know the small picture very well (we know how individual molecules should act), but when the system gets above a certain size everything goes out the window and we have to make very general guesses as to what will happen.

I did physics at university and still absolutely love it. I still read a lot of physics and maths.
